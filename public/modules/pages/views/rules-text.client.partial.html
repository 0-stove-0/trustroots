We want a world that encourages trust, adventure and intercultural connections. For that to happen through Trustroots we have written down some basic rules that apply to everyone. Please respect these rules:

  • Don't threaten or harass people.
  • Don't spam: keep our community clean from ads.
  • Don't automate: be a human being.
  • Respect copyrights: don't simply copy images and text from other people if you are not completely sure about the legal status.
  • Don't pretend to be someone else. It's okay to use an alias instead of your real name but don't create multiple profiles or use someone else's name.
  • Be yourself, helpful, kind, responsible, and respectful of others.

We try to keep our rules simple and we value transparency. We'll update these rules as needed.

See also our page about privacy.
