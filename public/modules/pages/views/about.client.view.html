This is early prototype of a travellers community for sharing and getting people together.

Travel anywhere and easily find great people who want to meet you as well. See where other travellers are and help each other out.

Ideas

Other social networks

By connecting to other social networks (e.g. Facebook), you can see where all your friends are on the map, what you have in common with people and show others who you are even without filling your profile or having many references.

The core team

Trustroots is being built by a small team of open-source developers and free economy activists who felt like the world or sharing is being taken over by corporations trying to monetize people's willingness to help each other. We are long time hospitality exchange enthusiasts with backgrounds in some other notable projects.

Communities

All users on the site belong to communities. For example you would have; "hitchhikers", "geeks", "vegans" etc. You could decide to be visible to all of them or only to some you really feel you belong to.

Searching only among hitchhikers community makes sense if you're on a hitchhiking trip.

Volunteering

We've always appreciated hard working, getting things done attitude and great ideas. We'll aim to keep volunteers entry level as low as possible. No bureaucracy. Pragmatic approach. Trust.

Free and open source forever

You can trust on us. We've been running other free and open projects for a decade now and we hope our deeds so far speak for us. This is not a startup.

Transparency & legal

We are about to form a legal organization behind Trustroots and we need your help for it!

Geeky stuff

We're huge fans of distributed networks, self hosted apps, API first approach etc. While it might not be the time to concentrate on this stuff just yet, it certainly gets us excited thinking all the possibilities.



Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

Trustroots seeks to be a platform for sharing and getting people together. We aim to connect likeminded people together, encourage intercultural connections and trust.

Vision

“A world that encourages trust and adventure.”

Values

  • Trust
  • Adventure
  • Transparency
  • Free software
